About us

At METABRICKS, we are a professionally managed full-fledged construction firm taking pride in working with each customer on a one-on-one basis;the personal service you'll enjoy will be a richly satisfying experience.

We provide premiere construction services and solutions to ensure that the management of your residential or commercial construction project goes smoothly and results in your total satisfaction.

The construction/alteration/interior design of a home is one of your most capital intensive investments. Therefore each tender is individually processed and professionally handled. We offer our clients the highest level of quality construction services at a fair and competitive price.

We can either work with your existing plans or provide you with the comprehensive design/build solution.
